Usage and Help
Purpose
Caffeine+ is meant to keep your PC awake during important operations. Unlike other programs, Caffeine+ DOES NOT emulate mouse movement, keyboard clicks, etc. Caffeine+ directly tells Windows to prevent sleep until Caffeine+ is turned off.
Basics
Launching Caffeine+ will add a coffee mug icon in the Windows tray.
Left-click the icon to toggle Caffeine on and off.
Right-click the icon to show the Options Menu.
Allow Display to Sleep
Check the "Allow Display to Sleep" box in the Options Menu to allow the screen to turn off while the system stays awake and continues operations.
Run at Startup
Check the "Run at Startup" box in the Options Menu to make Caffeine+ start with Windows.
Appearance
The "Appearance" tab in the Options Menu will open a sub-menu with different icon style names. Click on a style to apply it to the active tray icon.
Disable After
The "Disable After" tab in the Options Menu will open a sub-menu with different lengths of time. Click on a time to automatically switch Caffeine+ off after being on for that amount of time.
Quit
Press the quit button in the Options Menu to close Caffeine+
